Mission Statement

At Whitewater Rafting Co, our mission is to deliver unforgettable whitewater rafting adventures that combine excitement, safety, and a deep respect for nature. We strive to provide world-class river experiences for adventurers of all skill levels, led by expert guides and supported by a passionate team committed to environmental stewardship, customer service, and creating lifelong memories on the water.

Our History

Founded in 1987 by a band of thrill-seeking adventurers—a ranger with a love for rapids, a rogue avoiding walking, a barbarian who thought the river was a dragon, and a wizard testing waterproof spells—our company began as a quest to recover a legendary +1 Paddle of Buoyancy. What started as a chaotic float down a

wild river turned into a lifelong passion for guiding others through the rapids with courage, laughter, and the occasional bard song. Today, Whitewater Rafting Co proudly carries on that tradition, offering unforgettable whitewater adventures for everyone from first-level explorers to seasoned heroes—no saving throw required.
